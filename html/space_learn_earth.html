Distance


The distance of Earth from the Sun is defined as one astronomical unit (1 AU).

Protective Shield


Earth's atmosphere protects it from incoming meteoroids, most of which break up in the atmosphere before they can strike the surface.

Breathing Easy


Earth's atmosphere is 78 percent nitrogen, 21 percent oxygen and 1 percent other ingredients—the perfect balance to breathe and live.

Different Surfaces


Earth is a rocky planet with a solid and dynamic surface of mountains, canyons, plains and more. Most of our planet is covered in water.

Density


Earth is the densest planet on the solar system with the mean density of 5.5 g/cm3.
