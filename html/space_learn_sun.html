The age


The Sun is around 4.6 billion years old.

The mess


The sun holds 99.8% of the solar system's mass.

Hot Property

The surface temperature of the Sun is approximately 5,500°C and the temperature of the core around 15,700,000°C to allow fusion.

Biggest size


The Sun is 109 times the diameter of Earth and about one million Earths could fit inside it.

3rd most common


The Sun is a G - type main sequence star, the third most common type and accounting for roughly 7.5% of the known stars.

Supernova? Nope!


The Sun does not have the mass to explode as a supernova, instead it will form a red giant and then a white dwarf.

Source of life


Nothing could live on the Sun, but its energy is vital for most life on Earth.

Moonless existence


The Sun doe not have moons, but it is orbited by eight planets, at least five dwarf planets, tens of thousands of asteroids, and perhaps three trillion comets and icy bodies.
